Voca

Voice to text, instantly.

A macOS menu bar app for voice transcription. Double-tap Command to record, release to transcribe and auto-paste anywhere.

⌘ ⌘ to record
Download for Mac

Free and open source

View on GitHub Installation Guide

How it works

1

Double-tap Command

Press ⌘ twice quickly to start recording

2

Speak

Watch the waveform respond to your voice

3

Release

Text is transcribed and auto-pasted

Live Waveform

Animated floating overlay responds to your voice levels in real-time.

On-Device AI

CoreML models run locally. Your voice never leaves your Mac.

History

Access your recent transcriptions instantly with ⌃⌥V.

Auto-Paste

Transcribed text is automatically pasted wherever your cursor is.

Requires Apple Silicon and macOS 13.0+
